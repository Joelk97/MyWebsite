About me

Hi, I'm joel, a mechanical engineer from Switzerland.
I am currently doing my community service at the Fribourg University of Applied Sciences (HEIA), as a scientific associate.
During my training years I gained experience in product development in the area of tool holders and robot accessories and in creating software in the area of process optimization.
I am currently delving into the world of web development, web design, and generally into Python and JavaScript programming.
In my spare time I spend many hours riding my road bike.
